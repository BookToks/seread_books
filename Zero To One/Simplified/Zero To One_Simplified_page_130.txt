But that doesn’t show the weirdest thing about founders. Usually, we think that opposite traits can’t exist together. For example, a normal person can’t be both rich and poor at the same time. But this happens all the time with founders: startup CEOs might not have much cash but still look like millionaires on paper. 

They can switch between being grumpy and being super charming. Almost all successful entrepreneurs are both part of the group and also stand apart from it. When they do well, they get both fame and bad reputation. If you look at their traits, they seem to follow a strange pattern.
