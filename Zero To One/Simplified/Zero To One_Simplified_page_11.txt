They wanted to keep making progress. They dreamed of a four-day workweek, super cheap energy, and vacations on the moon. But that didn’t happen. 

The smartphones we use all the time keep us from noticing that the world around us is kind of old. The only things that have really changed a lot since the 1950s are computers and communication. 

That doesn’t mean our parents were wrong for hoping for a better future—they just thought it would happen automatically. Today, we need to imagine and create new technologies that can make the 21st century better and more peaceful than the 20th century.
