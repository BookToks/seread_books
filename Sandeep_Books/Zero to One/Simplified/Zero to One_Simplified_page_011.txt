People hoped for a four-day workweek, super cheap energy, and vacations on the moon. But none of that happened.

Smartphones keep us busy, but they also hide the fact that the world around us hasn’t changed much. Only computers and communication got a lot better since the middle of the last century.

That doesn’t mean our parents were wrong to dream of a better future—they just thought it would happen without much effort. Today, we need to both imagine and build new technologies that can make the 21st century more peaceful and successful than the 20th.
