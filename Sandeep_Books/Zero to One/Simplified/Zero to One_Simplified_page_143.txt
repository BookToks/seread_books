Because the world is so connected and modern weapons are super powerful, it’s hard not to wonder if a huge disaster could be stopped if it happened. This is why people are scared of the third possible situation: a collapse so bad that we wouldn’t survive it.

The last of the four possibilities is the hardest to picture: a super fast move toward a much better future. The result of this kind of breakthrough could look many different ways, but any one of them would be so different from today that it’s hard to explain.
