Can founders plan more than 20 to 30 years ahead? Even the smartest people can’t predict the far future. We don’t know the exact details, but we can guess the big picture.

Philosopher Nick Bostrom talks about four possible ways the future of humanity might go.

Long ago, people thought history was just a cycle of good times and bad times. Only recently have we hoped that bad times might end for good, but we still wonder if the peace we have now will last.

Most of the time, we push those worries away. Most people believe the world will end up at a steady level of development, like the richest countries today. If that happens, the future will be pretty similar to now.
