Leanness is a way of working, not a final goal. Making small changes to things you already have might help you improve a little, but it won’t help you find the best possible solution.

You could create the best app for ordering toilet paper on an iPhone. But just making small improvements won’t take you from nothing to something completely new.

A company is a weird place for someone who always expects things to get better without a plan. Why would you think your business will succeed if you don’t have a plan to make it happen?

In nature, survival of the fittest might work, but for startups, smart planning is what really helps.
