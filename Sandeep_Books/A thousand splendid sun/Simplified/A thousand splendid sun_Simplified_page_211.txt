But in Rasheed's eyes, she saw that both of them could be killed.

So Mariam lifted the shovel high above her head, as high as she could, bending it so it touched her lower back. She turned it so the sharp edge was straight up and down. As she did this, she realized it was the first time she was really making her own choice.
