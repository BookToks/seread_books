The shadow moved. Now there was a person crouching next to her, standing over her, and a voice whispered in her ear.

"You try this again, and I will find you. I swear on the Prophet's name that I will find you.

And when I do, no court in this broken country will stop me from what I will do. First to Mariam, then to her, and you last. I'll make you watch. Do you understand? I'll make you watch." Then he left the room, but not before kicking her side hard enough to hurt for days.
